## üìù Design System Summary Report: "Dockville" Parking

The design for "Dockville" uses a **clean, modern, and professional** design system focused on conveying **trust and security**. The overall aesthetic is highly functional, prioritizing clarity and user focus, typical of modern service-oriented websites.

### Key Principles:

1.  **Professionalism & Trust:** Achieved through the dominant use of a **Deep Navy Blue** color palette and high-contrast typography.
2.  **Clarity & Readability:** Excellent visual hierarchy is established through a generous use of **White** space and high contrast (white text on dark background, dark text on white background).
3.  **Action Orientation:** A strong, contrasting **Bright Blue** is reserved exclusively for primary Call-to-Action (CTA) elements, clearly guiding the user toward conversion.
4.  **Modernity:** The use of a **Sans-Serif font family** and simple, flat iconography gives the design a contemporary feel.

### Visual Hierarchy:

The layout uses large, bold headings and expansive dark sections to create immediate impact (Hero, Stats Bar). Content is broken into easily digestible blocks (Services, Testimonials) using a clean, card-based structure on a white background, ensuring a smooth, scannable user experience.
